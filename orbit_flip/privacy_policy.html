Orbit Flip — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-14

Orbit Flip ("the app") is published by Lance Games. This policy explains what information the app handles. The short version: the app collects no personal information and works fully offline, except for advertising described below.

Data we collect

None. The app has no accounts, no analytics, and no servers. Your scores and settings are stored only on your device and are deleted when you uninstall the app.

Advertising

The app shows ads served by Google AdMob. Ads are requested in non-personalized mode. To serve and measure ads, Google may process your device's advertising identifier and coarse technical data (such as device model and IP address). This data is processed by Google, not by us. See How Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services.

You can remove all advertising with the "Remove Ads" in-app purchase.

In-app purchases

Purchases are processed entirely by the App Store / Google Play. We never see your payment details.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13.
